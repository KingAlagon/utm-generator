📖 UTM Parameter Guide

What does each parameter mean?

utm_source Required
Where did this traffic come from?
Identifies which site, platform, or publisher sent the traffic to your page.
e.g. google, facebook, newsletter
utm_medium Required
What type of channel was it?
Describes the marketing medium or channel used to deliver the link.
e.g. cpc, email, social, organic
utm_campaign Required
What campaign is this for?
The name of your specific marketing campaign, promotion, or initiative.
e.g. spring_sale, product_launch
utm_content Optional
Which ad or link was clicked?
Used to differentiate between multiple links or ads in the same campaign — great for A/B testing.
e.g. hero_banner, sidebar_link
utm_term Optional
What keyword triggered this?
Used for paid search to identify which keyword the user searched before clicking your ad.
e.g. running+shoes, best+crm
